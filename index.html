About Us


We use hand woven fabrics sourced directly from weavers across the country. We combine these with artisanal process to create beautiful textiles. While giving close attention to every detail we believe in bringing out the beauty of handmade fabrics.


We work closely with master craftsmen from across the country to create unique products. Some of the crafts that we specialise in are Kalamkari, Dabu, Hand Batik, Kantha, Ajrak, Aplique, Shiboris etc


We also have an in house facility for Block Printing in Mumbai, where a team of highly skilled artisans are developing new designs everyday. They make beautiful Block Prints and hand stitched shbori>

We make sarees, dress materials, yardages and dupattas on fabrics like tussar, chanderi, maheshwaris, mulberry, linen and kotas
